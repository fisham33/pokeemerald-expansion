## Documentation Strategy

1. **Phase 1**: Complete all starter lines (24 lines = ~72 Pokemon)
2. **Phase 2**: Document all legendary/mythical Pokemon (~80-90 Pokemon)
3. **Phase 3**: Add pseudo-legendaries and popular competitive Pokemon (~50-60 Pokemon)
4. **Phase 4**: Fill in remaining base forms as needed
5. **Phase 5**: Alternate forms (can often reuse base form palettes)

**Total High Priority**: ~220-250 Pokemon
**With Medium Priority**: ~350-400 Pokemon

After completing high priority Pokemon, most developers will have sufficient reference material for the majority of species they care about configuring.
